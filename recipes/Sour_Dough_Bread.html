Sour Dough Bread Recipe

Want to learn how to make sourdough bread at home the easy way? Our beginners sourdough bread tutorial will guide you through the process, step-by-step.

Ingredients

  • Bread Flour
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Sourdough Starter

Steps

  1. Feed your sourdough starter and let rise until doubled
  2. Mix the dough and let rest for 1 hour at room temperature
  3. Perform 2 sets of stretch and folds 30 minutes apart
  4. Cover the dough and let rise for 1-2 hours
  5. Score and bake
