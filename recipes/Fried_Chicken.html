Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Brine
  • Buttermilk
  • Coating
  • Oil

Steps

Preparing and Brining the Chicken

  1. Combine brine ingredients
  2. Boil for 1 minute
  3. Remove from heat
  4. Marinate chicken for 8 - 12 hours
  5. Remove the chicken
  6. Pat chicken dry

Breading the Chicken

  1. Whisk together breading ingredients
  2. Divide the mixture between two bowls
  3. Pour buttermilk into bowl
  4. Make the shaggy dredging
  5. Dip chicken in the order which everything was created

Cooking the Chicken

  1. Dip the thighs
  2. Carefully lower the thighs
  3. Fry for 2 minutes
  4. Transfer and let rest
